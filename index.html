Assumptions

Calculations are based on an average reading speed of 238 words per minute and a typical book of 200 pages at 300 words per page (60,000 words), which takes approximately 4.2 hours to read. These are rough averages — your actual reading speed, book length, and comprehension time will vary. Treat the results as a fun estimation, not a scientific measurement.
